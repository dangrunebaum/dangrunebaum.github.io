As an individual who helped "Asian America" grow by sponsoring my Japanese wife's naturalization as a new American citizen, this project has personal motivations. Asian Americans now constitute the United States' fastest growing ethnic minority, and are on track to become the nation's largest immigrant group. This interactive website utilizes U.S. Census Bureau data to explore their ethnic and spatial distribution as well as the growth patterns of Asian Americans. Visit the website.
