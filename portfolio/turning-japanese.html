Turning Japanese

As we enter the Asian century, "Turning Japanese" investigates how Japan became a cultural giant by tracing the journey of Japanese loanwords into English through the Oxford English Dictionary, New York Times and Google Search. Viewers can interact with Japanese loanwords to understand the process whereby Japanese concepts are translated into English, and become part of the Western conversation. This project constituted the final thesis for the Master of Science in Data Visualization program at Parsons School of Design. (JavaScript, D3, Vue) View project  View video
