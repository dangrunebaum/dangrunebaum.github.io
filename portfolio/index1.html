Dan Grunebaum Work About

I am a data visualization developer and journalist with a deep background in print, web and broadcast media. A graduate of the Master of Science in Data Visualization program at Parsons School of Design in New York, my visualizations and writing explore material from cultural geographies to content analytics and brand interactions. I'm inspired by the new possibilities for understanding our changing world afforded by the explosion of data launched by the Web. A onetime Japan resident and editor of Tokyo's Metropolis magazine, and contributor to the New York Times, Christian Science Monitor and Rolling Stone, I'm especially interested in the growing importance of Asian culture to the Western conversation, and how rising Asian immigration is transforming America. See more editorial and broadcast work at tokyo-flow.com.
