Created to celebrate the earth’s population reaching 8 billion humans, this globe visualizes the planet’s populace by country and continent, helping users to understand the distribution of people around the earth’s many regions. On the right, a population counter bar keeps a running total that grows as the population of countries and continents accumulates. On the left, filters highlight nations with populations of under 1 million, 1-10 million, 10-100 million, and over 100 million. Built with GEOJSON map data from Natural Earth and downloaded from GEOJSON Maps. Population figures are from 2019. Visit the website.
