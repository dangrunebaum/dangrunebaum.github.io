WHERE ARE PEOPLE THE FREEST?

  

The 2018 Human Freedom Index presents a broad measure of human freedom, including 79 indicators of personal and economic freedom ranging from rule of law to security, religion, freedom of expression, property rights, trade and regulation. 162 countries for which adequate data are available are rated on a 0-10 scale, while some countries are tied for position. The above chart provides a countdown of the nations from #162 to #1, while the below chart represents countries individually. The Human Freedom Index is published by the Fraser Institute, the Liberales Institut at the Friedrich Naumann Foundation for Freedom, and the Cato Institute.
