What is the cultural geography of New York City? What, for example, is the distribution of music, museums or movie organizations? Is there more dance in Brooklyn or the Bronx? This visualization maps over 2,000 organizations in the Department of Cultural Affairs directory to paint a picture of the city's cultural landscape. Color-coding shows the distribution of 18 disciplines across New York, and which disciplines boast the most organizations. Built with JavaScript, jQuery, Leaflet and p5.js. Source: NYC Open Data.
